3DHOP Viewer Help

Mouse Controls

Rotate the model by pressing your left mouse button and dragging.

Zoom in and out using your mouse scroll wheel.


How to Measure

Click to enable measuring. Click once on the first point you'd like to measure and then click on the second point where you would like to stop measuring. The measurement will be displayed to the right of the icon. Click to turn it off.


How to Pick a Point

Click to enable point picking. Click a point on the surface of the 3D model, and you will see the XYZ position of the point picked displayed to the right of the icon. Click to turn it off.


How to Section the Model

Click to open up the "sectioning" interface. Clicking on one of the three buttons on the left activates/deactivates the X, Y and Z sectioning plane, the three sliders moves the corresponding sectioning plane across the extent of the scene. The "flip" checkboxes invert the direction of the corresponding sectioning plane. The two checkboxes in the lower part of the interface toggles the visibility of the sectioning planes and of the highlighted border. Click to turn it off.


How to Change Perspective

Click to switch to orthographic projection (typically used in technical drawings). Click to switch to perspective projection (similar to human sight).


How to Toggle Fullscreen

Click on the icon to toggle fullscreen mode.
